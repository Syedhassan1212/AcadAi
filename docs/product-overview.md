# AcadAI — Your Personalized Academic Operating System

AcadAI (Adaptive Study OS) is not just a study app; it is a **self-evolving intelligence system** designed to maximize your learning efficiency. By converting your static lecture notes, textbooks, and PDFs into a dynamic, interactive ecosystem, AcadAI ensures you study the right material, at the right time, with the right support.

---

## 🌟 Core Value Proposition
Traditional studying is linear and passive. AcadAI makes it **dynamic and active**. 
- **Passive**: Reading a PDF and hoping it sticks.
- **Active (AcadAI)**: Uploading a PDF, seeing it broken into a dependency-aware "Study Map," and having an AI tutor guide you through the hardest parts while tracking your mental fatigue.

---

## 🛠️ What the App Does: Feature Deep-Dive

### 1. The Architect: Intelligent Material Ingestion
When you upload a file (PDF, DOCX, etc.), AcadAI's "Architect" goes to work. Using **Gemini 3 Flash**, it doesn't just read the text; it understands the structure:
- **Topic Extraction**: Automatically identifies the main subjects and sub-concepts.
- **Dependency Mapping**: Determines which concepts you need to know *before* moving on to more advanced ones.
- **Task Generation**: Breaks down massive chapters into bite-sized, 45-minute executable tasks.

### 2. The Map: Dependency-Aware Study Graph (DAG)
The heart of AcadAI is the **Study Map**. Instead of a simple list, your curriculum is visualized as a network (DAG):
- **Visual Path**: See exactly how Topic A leads to Topic B.
- **Topological Readiness**: The system "locks" advanced topics until you've mastered their prerequisites.
- **Critical Path Highlighting**: It visually highlights the "must-study" path to hit your deadlines.

### 3. The Coach: Dynamic Daily Planning
AcadAI acts as a personal coach. Every morning, it generates a **Daily Plan** tailored to you:
- **Smart Prioritization**: It calculates what you should study based on urgency, difficulty, and your past procrastination habits.
- **Ready-to-Go**: It only serves tasks you are "ready" for (prerequisites cleared).
- **Proactive Replanning**: If you fail a task or miss a deadline, the AI automatically reshuffles your entire schedule to keep you on track.

### 4. The Neural Tutor: Context-Aware AI Support
No more getting stuck for hours. The **Neural Tutor** is built into every focus session:
- **Subject Mastery**: It knows everything about the files you've uploaded.
- **Empathy & Urgency**: It tracks your "Health Score." If it sees you're struggling, it becomes more encouraging and simplifies concepts.
- **Active Recall**: Generates quizzes and summaries on the fly based on your current focus.

### 5. The Pulse: Performance & Health Monitoring
AcadAI monitors your "Academic Health" using advanced data science (CUSUM trends):
- **Burnout Detection**: If your focus sessions are getting longer but your success rate is dropping, the system flags "Burnout Risk."
- **Consistency Tracking**: Rewards you for "Deep Work" streaks.
- **Weak Area Targeting**: Automatically identifies topics where you struggle and boosts their priority in your plan.

## 🚀 The User Journey: How to Use AcadAI

### Phase 1: Onboarding
Upload your syllabus, lecture slides, or textbook chapters. Within seconds, AcadAI generates your entire learning path.

### Phase 2: Exploration
Navigate the **Study Map**. View the dependencies, read AI-generated summaries, and see your projected completion dates.

### Phase 3: Deep Work
Start a **Focus Session**. The UI transforms into a distraction-free environment with a countdown timer, a notepad, and the Neural Tutor sidebar.

### Phase 4: Feedback & Evolution
At the end of every session, you log how you felt. Did you find it hard? Did you get distracted? AcadAI's **Experience Replay** engine uses this data to make your *next* plan even more accurate.
